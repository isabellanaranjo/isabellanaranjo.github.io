"A WORK OF ART IS A SCREAM OF FREEDOM"
For as long as I can remember I always knew I wanted to create art. As a little girl I always admired my dad's artwork and how it always brought a huge smile onto my face.  From then on I knew that I wanted to touch people's hearts with my art. I never really knew what field I wanted to go into until my last year in middle school when I started to apply for high school. In my freshman year in highschool I started using software like Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop. I even dwelled into 3D design and basic website designing. By the end of my freshman year I even got my first certification Adobe Illustrator.  I dont know where life will take me but I hope people see my name printed in either movie credits or on a magazine as the artistic director.
